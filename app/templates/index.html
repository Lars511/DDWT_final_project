Meet & Go Groningen

Meet & Go Groningen is a social platform designed to help people in Groningen connect through shared activities. Whether you are new to the city or simply looking to meet others with similar interests, Meet & Go Groningen makes it easy to find and organise activities together.

Users can browse activities created by others, join events that match their interests, or create their own activities. Activities are organised by categories and activity types, making it simple to explore what is happening in and around Groningen.

The goal of Meet & Go Groningen is to lower the barrier to social interaction by bringing people together around everyday activities such as sports, studying, walking, or leisure events.
